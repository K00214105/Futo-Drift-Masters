FUTO DRIFT MASTERS

Introduction

Welcome to Futo Drift Masters. We are a Grand Theft Auto online drifting crew who are devoted to drifting the beautiful vehicle called the "Karin Futo". Established in Janury 2014, the crew consists of many chill and talented drifters. We are always sideways in our favourite car in the game. We create exciting drifting montages for our YouTube channel and are always practicing our skills on our custom drift tracks.

Youtube Series

Futo Drift Masters features a range of exciting, action packed drifting videos which get uploaded on our YouTube channel and Instagram profile for the world to see. Futo Drift Masters has new exciting projects up it's sleaves. We now have three video editors on our team to bring a little diversity to the table. Make sure and subscribe to the YouTube channel to ensure you keep up with our sideway drifting antics.

Photoshop Edits

Our PhotoShop edits have recieved a good response on our Instagram account and we really love the results. Chronic our crew leader likes to get creative with these edits and they really get peoples attention. Hopefully RockStar will one day give us the freedom to modify our vechicles in such ways as seen in these edits. Imagine taking the Futo to Bennys, that would be a dream come true for Futo Drift Masters.
